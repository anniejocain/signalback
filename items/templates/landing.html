SignalBack helps collaborators collect and share their web discoveries.

How it works

Easy Steps

One The first time you use SignalBack you'll add the SignalBack button to your browser. Your collaborators do the same.

Two When you're browsing the Web and discover a cool link, click on the SignalBack button. A snapshot of your link will be added to a stream you share with your collaborators.

Three SignalBack automatically pushes links added to your stream out to Twitter, your blog, and just about anywhere else you choose using the SignalBack API.

Why you need it
